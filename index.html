Explore AlUla

AlUla is one of Saudi Arabia’s most fascinating historical destinations. The region is famous for its breathtaking desert landscapes, ancient civilizations, and remarkable archaeological sites.

Visitors from around the world come to AlUla to experience the beauty of the desert, explore ancient tombs carved into sandstone mountains, and learn about the history of civilizations that lived in the region thousands of years ago.

Famous Landmarks

AlUla contains many famous landmarks that attract tourists and researchers alike. Some of the most popular sites include:

  • Hegra (Madain Saleh)
  • Elephant Rock
  • AlUla Old Town
  • Dadan Archaeological Site

Why Visit AlUla

AlUla combines history, culture, and natural beauty. Visitors can enjoy guided heritage tours, desert adventures, cultural festivals, and traditional markets.

The region continues to grow as one of the most important tourism projects in Saudi Arabia while preserving its unique cultural heritage and historical treasures.
