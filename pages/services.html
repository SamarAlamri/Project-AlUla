Explore AlUla Your Way

Discover AlUla through carefully designed themed Tour that combine multiple experiences into one seamless trip. Each Tour follows a planned schedule of activities, allowing you to explore the destination without the stress of planning. Choose between full-day or half-day trips based on your time and interests.

Heritage Discovery Tour

Step into the past and explore AlUla’s most iconic historical landmarks through a guided cultural experience.

  • Visit Hegra (UNESCO World Heritage Site)
  • Explore Dadan archaeological site
  • Walk through AlUla Old Town

Duration: Full Day / Half Day

Best for: History lovers and cultural explorers

Adventure & Nature Tour

Experience the thrill of AlUla’s landscapes with exciting outdoor activities in the desert.

  • Desert hiking trails
  • Rock climbing experience
  • Hot air balloon ride
  • Desert camping

Duration: Full Day

Best for: Adventure seekers and nature lovers

Culture & Arts Tour

Immerse yourself in AlUla’s vibrant cultural scene through art, festivals, and local traditions.

  • Visit art exhibitions
  • Attend seasonal festivals
  • Explore traditional markets

Duration: Half Day / Evening

Best for: Culture lovers and relaxed experiences

Relaxed Highlights Tour

A shorter journey designed for visitors who want a light and enjoyable introduction to AlUla.

  • Visit selected landmarks

  • Café or rest stop experience
